==============================================================
MULTILATERAL TARIFF AMENDMENT AND OVERRIDE ACT (ACT-20220415)
==============================================================
Ratification Date: 2022-04-15 | Effective Date: 2022-04-15

Be it resolved by the signatory nations that the following modifications and overrides to existing bilateral agreements are officially codified and active as of the effective date.

Amendment Clause 1: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Delta on Textiles from Nation Beta is hereby modified to 3.1%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 2: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Gamma on HeavyEquipment from Nation Delta is hereby modified to 5.4%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 3: Article 32 of the Treaty between Nation Gamma and Nation Delta is modified. The MFN floor is adjusted to 1.5% and the margin is set to 1.0%.
