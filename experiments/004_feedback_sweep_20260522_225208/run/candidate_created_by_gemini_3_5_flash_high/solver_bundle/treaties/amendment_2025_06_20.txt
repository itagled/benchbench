==============================================================
MULTILATERAL TARIFF AMENDMENT AND OVERRIDE ACT (ACT-20250620)
==============================================================
Ratification Date: 2025-06-20 | Effective Date: 2025-06-20

Be it resolved by the signatory nations that the following modifications and overrides to existing bilateral agreements are officially codified and active as of the effective date.

Amendment Clause 1: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Alpha on Agriculture from Nation Delta is hereby modified to 5.0%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 2: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Epsilon on HeavyEquipment from Nation Beta is hereby modified to 5.3%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 3: Article 32 of the Treaty between Nation Beta and Nation Gamma is modified. The MFN floor is adjusted to 0.5% and the margin is set to 0.3%.
