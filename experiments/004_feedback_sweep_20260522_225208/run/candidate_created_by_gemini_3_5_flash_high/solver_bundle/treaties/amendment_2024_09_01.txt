==============================================================
MULTILATERAL TARIFF AMENDMENT AND OVERRIDE ACT (ACT-20240901)
==============================================================
Ratification Date: 2024-09-01 | Effective Date: 2024-09-01

Be it resolved by the signatory nations that the following modifications and overrides to existing bilateral agreements are officially codified and active as of the effective date.

Amendment Clause 1: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Beta on Agriculture from Nation Gamma is hereby modified to 3.3%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 2: The base tariff rate applied by Nation Delta on Textiles from Nation Alpha is hereby modified to 5.8%, overriding all previous article clauses.
Amendment Clause 3: Article 32 of the Treaty between Nation Beta and Nation Gamma is modified. The MFN floor is adjusted to 0.6% and the margin is set to 0.1%.
